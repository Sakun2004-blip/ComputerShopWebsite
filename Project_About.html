Bio

Alpha-X Computers (Pvt Ltd) is a dynamic technology enterprise located in Maharagama, Colombo 6, Sri Lanka. Founded with the vision to deliver cutting-edge computing solutions to both home users and business organisations, the company has built a reputation for reliability, expertise and innovation in the local IT market.

From its first day, Alpha-X Computers has committed to offering high-quality hardware, software and support services — whether that means custom-built PCs, effective repairs, or upgrades to keep customers ahead of technological change. The team understands that in today’s fast-moving digital world, staying current matters

At the heart of the company’s mission is the principle of partnership: not simply selling products, but collaborating with clients to understand their requirements, recommend appropriate solutions, and provide ongoing service. The goal is to be a trusted advisor, not just a supplier

Products and services at Alpha-X Computers span the full spectrum of computing needs. From everyday desktop PCs and laptops, to gaming rigs, servers, networking equipment and peripherals — the company ensures that customers receive equipment from reputable brands, properly configured and supported.

Equally important is the after-sales support. Alpha-X Computers offers maintenance, diagnostics, upgrade paths and reliable service so that clients feel confident their technology investment is protected and optimised over time. This extends to businesses, educational institutions and individual users alike.

In the Sri Lankan marketplace, where technology demands are diverse and resources sometimes constrained, Alpha-X Computers stands out by providing transparent pricing, honest advice and practical solutions-—-without unnecessary complexity. Simplicity and clarity are core values.

Internally, the company invests in staying current: the team is continually training, sourcing new hardware platforms and mastering emerging technologies such as high-performance cooling, SSD/NVMe architectures, advanced graphics cards, and bespoke build options. This ensures the company can meet sophisticated client requireme

The brand identity of Alpha-X Computers reflects a fusion of “Alpha” (the first, the leader) with “X” (the variable, the innovation) — symbolising the company’s aim to be both foundational and forward-looking in the computing domain. Through its logo, storefront, and digital presence, the company communicates professionalism and tech-savviness

Looking ahead, Alpha-X Computers aims to expand its footprint across Colombo and beyond, exploring partnerships, reseller opportunities, and perhaps online build-customisation services. The ambition is to become a top destination for computing excellence in the region

Above all, the company values remain grounded in integrity, customer-centred service and continual improvement. Whether an individual wanting a reliable laptop, or a business needing a network upgrade, Alpha-X Computers (Pvt Ltd) promises a straightforward, trustworthy and innovative path to meet technology needs.
